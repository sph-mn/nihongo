word search
  • contains over 30000 translations, with words sorted by frequency of use. it is designed to cover the most essential words, not to serve as a comprehensive dictionary
  • when searching by romaji, the tool uses a fuzzy search and finds words with similar spelling and pronunciation. this is useful if you have heard a word but are unsure about details like short versus long vowels or whether it contains a "z" or "s" - this search may still find the word you are looking for
  • supports regular expressions. for example, ^word searches at the beginning, word$ searches at the end, and . can be used as a wildcard
  • "word for searching exactly as typed
  • katakana words (~8000) are not included. they seem distracting if included
  • the length of result words is limited relative to the length of the input word, preventing short word searches from returning many longer words that contain it
  • everything is contained in this html file. the file can be copied, used offline, modified, and rehosted under the cc-by-sa license
  • translations are sourced from jmdict, word frequency data is sourced from gimenes, m., & new, b. (2015) wordlex
character search
  • the jouyou characters sorted by stroke order
  • hover or tap over character shows meaning and readings
  • multiple characters, parts of meanings, and multiple comma-separated parts of meanings can be used in the search field
  • graphics and stroke order from kanjivg
